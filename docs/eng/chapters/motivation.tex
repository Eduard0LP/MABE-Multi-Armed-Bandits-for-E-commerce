\chapter{Motivation and objectives}\label{ch:motivation}

The reason for the choice of the multi-armed bandit problem as the topic of this
master's thesis is to carry out a study of a field of machine learning (reinforcement
learning) that is normally not dealt with in much detail when one begins to study
machine learning and artificial intelligence, since unsupervised learning, and above
all supervised learning, usually take up more of the focus due to their greater number
of applications in different areas.  

The main objective of this work consists in showing the usefulness of the theoretical
framework of multi-armed bandits for the resolution of real-world problems, such as
the design of a recommender system for an e-commerce platform similar to what was done
in \textcite{rohde2018}, which will be showed in the practical part of this document.  

The multi-armed bandit and contextual multi-armed bandit problems are problems that
are usually studied from an academic point of view as a first example of reinforcement
learning, but their transfer to real-world environments is almost never studied. These
problems, despite their apparent simplicity, give rise to a broad and complex field of
study, and whose application in business environments can have a high added value.
